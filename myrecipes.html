B

Broccoli Feta Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 liter of chicken or vegetable broth or 4 cups of homemade broth
  • 2 Heads of broccoli (or one bag of frozen broccoli)
  • 3–4 Big garlic cloves
  • 2 sticks of celery (around 150 grams)
  • 125 grams of feta
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. On medium heat, add the oil and butter.
  2. Roughly chop onions, garlic, and celery. Cook until onions are translucent. Add broccoli and cook briefly.
  3. Cover veggies with broth, bring to a boil, then simmer 30 minutes.
  4. Add feta, salt, and pepper in the last 5 minutes.
  5. Blend and enjoy!

R

Kalbi Ribs

Ingredients

  • 1 Rack of ribs
  • ½ Jar of premade Kalbi sauce
  • 3 inches of fresh ginger
  • 4–5 cloves of fresh garlic
  • Green onions to taste
  • 1 White onion
  • 4 Celery stalks
  • 2 Tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon chili garlic paste

Steps

  1. Roughly cut green onions, white onion, celery, garlic, and ginger.
  2. Add vegetables, Kalbi sauce, soy sauce, and chili garlic paste to a large bag.
  3. Remove membrane from ribs and place ribs in the marinade bag.
  4. Marinate 4–24 hours.
  5. Preheat oven to 250°F.
  6. Place ribs and marinade on a foil‑lined baking sheet.
  7. Cook 3–4 hours, flipping every 30 minutes.
  8. Broil 2–3 minutes uncovered.
  9. Enjoy!

S

Sourdough Loaf

Ingredients

  • 125 grams starter
  • 325 grams water
  • 500 grams flour or bread flour
  • 10 grams salt
  • Rice flour (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix starter, water, salt, and flour. Rest 1 hour.
  2. Do 4 sets of stretch‑and‑folds, 30 minutes apart.
  3. Shape loaf, rest 15 minutes.
  4. Place in banneton, cover, refrigerate overnight or up to 3 days.
  5. Preheat oven to 450°F.
  6. Score bread and place in Dutch oven on parchment.
  7. Bake 40 minutes covered, then 10–15 minutes uncovered.
  8. Cool 1–2 hours before slicing.
